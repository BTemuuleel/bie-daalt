OVERLORD

General Rating | 4.8

Overlord is an action-packed fantasy anime
series that centers around a popular online
game, Yggdrasil, which suddenly becomes a
real world after the game's servers shut
down. The story follows a powerful
undead sorcerer named Momonga, who was
once a player in Yggdrasil and finds
himself trapped in this new reality along
with his loyal NPC servants.
