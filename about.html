About

Overlord is an action-packedfantasy anime series that centers around a popular anime
game, Yggdrasil which suddenly becomes a real world after the game's servers shut
down. The story follows a powerful undead sorcerer named Momonga, who was once a
plyaer in Yggdrasil and finds himself trapped in this new realty along with his loyal
NPC servants.

Throughout the series, Momonga sets out to conquer this new world while attempting to
uncover the truth behind this mysterious transformation. One of the strenghts of Overlord
is it's attention to detail in the world-building and character development, which makes the
story feel immersive and engaging.

The animation is also top-notch, with beautifully rendered action scenes and character
designs. Overall, Overlord is a must-watch for fans of the fantasy and action genres. It
offers a unique take on the "trapped in a game" trope and presents a compelling story
that will keep viewers on the edge of their seats.
